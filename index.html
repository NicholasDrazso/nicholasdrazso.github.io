About Me

I am a 2nd year Mechatronics Engineering Student at the University of Waterloo. I am passionate about robotics and mechatronics specifically because it is a multidisplinary field where I can use a mix of skills to come up with creative solutions to complex problems. My most recent internship was at BotBuilt, a robotics start up located in North Carolina, USA. At BotBuilt, I contributed to the expansion of the company through long days of mechanical, pneumatic, and electrical assembly. Additionally, I spearheaded and assisted with many R&D projects where I designed, tested, and implemented mechanical, pneumatic, electrical, and software components.


Moving forward, I am looking to strengthen my software skills and also look to purchase my own 3D printer to begin doing more complex personal projects. Additionally, on the side I make custom wooden engraved pens and I run this as a small business. One day I hope to have my own company, that is somehow making the world a better place. In the mean time, through my internships and projects, I hope to keep learning skills that can help lead to this goal.
