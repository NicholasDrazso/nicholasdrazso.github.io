About Me

I am a 4th year Mechatronics Engineering Student at the University of Waterloo but I am also a 2 time founder with 5 internships completed (currently doing #6) and I am also a design team lead. I am passionate about humanoid robotics and mechatronics specifically because it is a multidisplinary field where I can use a mix of skills to come up with creative solutions to complex problems. I love the idea of there not being a textbook or 10000 versions of something done before to copy off of, and I must use first principles or a series of educated guesses followed with testing to come up with solutions. I love to build, create, and I have a passion for entrepreneurship/startups.


I am currently looking for full time work or a 7th and final internship which will hopefully be in the field of actuator design, sensor design, or design/testing for novel systems.
