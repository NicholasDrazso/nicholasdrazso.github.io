Page under construction. I loved tinkering and troubleshooting with my own paintball equipment and couldnt afford to get it repaired by other companies so I learned to do it myself. I became pretty good at it and realized there was an opportunity to start servicing other peoples equipment and make money. I approached my local field and started doing it there when I was 11 then after about 1 season it really blew up and I started to make a name for myself. I then upgraded to bigger fields and bigger events and got some friends and trained them as employees. After 2 seasons, at 14 years old, I was servicing events up to 1000 people with 3 employees under a tent. It became pretty successful until COVID shut down most of the fields within a 3h drive and I got busy with university, so I officially stopped around 2019 but this was my first taste of real entrepreneurship and it made me realize how in love with this I was! In the future I hope to follow a passion of mine and make my own company around it!
